Ilias Panayiotaros, Golden Dawn MP
General Information
  • far-right political party in Athens, Greece
  • led by Nikolaos Michaloliakos
  • founded on January 1st, 1985; 34 years ago
  • has gained an increase in support over the years, particularly in 2012, when they ran a campaign based on concerns about unemployment, the economy, and immigration
Values
  • Eurosceptic, anti-globalist
  • nationalist
  • promotes the idea of a Greater Greece through the expansion of Greek territory
  • opposes non-European, and particularly Muslim, immigration into the mainly Greek areas of southern Greece and Athens
  • aspires to combat Islam in the region
