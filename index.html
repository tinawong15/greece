GOLDEN DAWN


EXPLORE



Who are they?

“So we can rid this land of filth.”

Golden Dawn campaign slogan, 2012
Overview
  • far-right political party headquartered in Athens, Greece
  • led by Nikolaos Michaloliakos
  • founded on January 1st, 1985; 34 years ago
  • has shown an admiration for past Greek dictators and for figures in Nazi Germany
  • has a strict hierarchical structure, with local chapters following orders from the central party leadership
  • has gained an increase in support over the years, particularly after the 2008-09 global financial crisis
  • has been involved in many cases of violence and vandalism
Ideology and Values
  • Eurosceptic, anti-globalist
  • nationalist, believes in the “cultural superiority” of an “Orthodox Greek nation”
  • social conservative
  • anti-Semitic
  • considers the United States and liberalism to be the largest threat to the nation
  • opposes non-European, and particularly Muslim, immigration into the mainly Greek areas of southern Greece and Athens
  • contributes to combating Islam in the region
