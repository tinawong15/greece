Causes of Conflict:

Police

The government has been reluctant to address the violence perpetrated against immigrants. When Athens residents seek help from the police, authorities direct them to the Golden Dawn to find a "solution" for what is seen as an "immigrant problem." Thusly, the Golden Dawn was able to run on a twin platform for the 2012 general elections: opposition to immigration and condemnation of the bailouts. To this day, the police remain a conservative enclave.

Detention Centers

Recently, Panousis has pledged to shut down the detention centers that housed immigrants in appalling conditions for the conservative government iniative Xenios Zeus, which had been condemned as inhumane by the UN. It serves as a reminder of the Right's determination to use fear of crime and immigration to muster support among an insecure populace.
